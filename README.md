A production-ready **school management system** built with **React + Supabase**, designed to support modern language schools and educational businesses.

This platform centralizes student administration, live and recorded lessons, billing, CRM pipelines, and automated communication workflows in a single scalable dashboard.

---

## Overview

This project was developed to manage the complete lifecycle of an online and hybrid school, including:

- Live classes (group or private)
- In-person lesson scheduling
- Recorded course content delivery
- Subscription and billing control
- Student progress tracking
- CRM automation and WhatsApp communication

---

## Core Features

### Student & School Administration
- Student registration and profile management  
- Class organization and scheduling  
- Role-based dashboards for admins, teachers, and students  

### Courses & Learning Content
- Support for live classes, in-person lessons, and recorded modules  
- Course material and document delivery inside the platform  
- Lesson creation and editing workflows  

### Dashboards & Analytics
- Admin overview panel with KPIs  
- Financial dashboard for payments and revenue visibility  
- Student dashboard with learning progress tracking  

### Billing & Subscription Management
- Payment status monitoring  
- Billing automation and overdue control  
- Subscription-based access logic  

### Built-in CRM System
- Contact and lead pipeline management  
- Kanban-style deal tracking  
- Tags, notes, stages, and responsible user assignment  

### WhatsApp Automation
- Scheduled messages and automated responses  
- Communication workflows for billing reminders and student support  
- Integration panel for message management
